## Phase Goal

**As a** self-hosted single-user monai owner, **I want to** move between the Chat,
Cashflow, Investments, and Settings pages from a shared nav bar without full page
reloads, **so that** the app feels like one coherent multi-page product instead of a
single screen.

> Derived from the ROADMAP Phase 3 `**Goal:**` line ("Users can navigate between all
> pages of the app and configure it from the browser") and Phase 3 success criteria 1–2.
> The navigation half of the goal is delivered here; the configuration half is delivered
> by plans 03-02 and 03-03.
